Our Story

Pacific Trails Resort began with one goal: build a peaceful coastal retreat where adventure and comfort can exist together. What started as a small family vision has grown into a place where guests can reset, explore, and reconnect with nature.

Guest Promise

Each stay is designed for comfort, flexibility, and support. We help guests choose activities that match their pace, from peaceful mornings to full days on the trail.

Trail Culture

Our team believes the best trips blend adventure and rest. We share local tips, maintain safe routes, and make sure your schedule includes both movement and downtime.

Coastal Community

Pacific Trails supports nearby businesses and works to protect the natural beauty around us so future visitors can enjoy the same unforgettable views.

About the Trail Team

Our hosts and guides are campers, hikers, and coast-lovers who know the local terrain and weather patterns well. We support every guest with practical planning, clear trail recommendations, and flexible activity options for all experience levels.

When you arrive, we focus on helping you feel prepared and at home, from your first morning coffee to your final sunset walk.

What We Value Most

  • Time in nature should feel easy and restorative.
  • Hospitality starts with listening to each guest.
  • Great vacations balance comfort, adventure, and connection.
  • Every trail should be respected and protected.

Join Our Family

Many guests return each year because the experience feels personal. We remember preferences, celebrate milestones, and keep improving the stay so each visit feels both familiar and new.
